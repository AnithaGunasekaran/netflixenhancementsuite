Chromecast shortcut

To play something on Netflix on your Chromecast you have to click to play, then click the Chromecast icon and then choose the Chromecast device.

While there's no way around the Chromecast popup, you can now have the play buttons on the Netflix homepage replaced with a Chromecast button which sets things up for you a bit faster.
